Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with egg, hard cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's a simple yet flavorful dish that is quick to prepare and perfect for a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Egg yolks
  • Black pepper
  • salt
  • Spaghetti
  • Pecorino romano

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, cook the guanciale until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together egg yolks, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with guanciale, removing from heat.
  5. Quickly mix in the egg mixture, stirring until creamy and well combined.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper on top.
