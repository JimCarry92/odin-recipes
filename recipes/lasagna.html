Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the meat and onion in a skillet until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and spices, simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
